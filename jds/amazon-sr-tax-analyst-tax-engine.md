## Description

The Tax Engine team is hiring a Sr. Indirect Tax expert with deep Brazil expertise to join our Global Tax Calculation Services team based in Sao Paulo. The Global Tax Calculation Services Program is comprised of tax professionals and software engineers that are responsible for providing end-to-end global transaction tax calculation solutions (sales and use taxes, value-added taxes, good and services taxes, and other consumption taxes) to Amazon and thousands of other merchants selling on Amazon marketplaces and other e-commerce platforms.

The individual in this role will contribute critical indirect tax research and product classification work for Brazil's complex indirect tax landscape. This position will primarily support Brazil marketplace operations, focusing on Indirect Tax ICMS research, conducting comprehensive taxability research across all 27 states, analyzing intrastate and interstate scenarios and Brazil Tax Reform implementation (CBS/IBS/IS) during reform period. This position will require configuration and ongoing maintenance of Amazon's transaction tax calculation software. It will also require work closely with local business partners, Tax Planning and rest of members of the tax department providing guidance and support to Global Product Classification.

This position is office-based. Amazon encourages open communication about unique needs for flexible arrangements.

### Key job responsibilities

- Conducting comprehensive ICMS tax research across all 27 Brazilian states, including intrastate and interstate rate scenarios
- Determining CST (Tax Situation Code) and cClassTrib (Tax Classification Code) combinations as mandatory inputs for government Tax Calculator API
- Supporting dual tax regime operations during the 2026-2032 transition period
- Supporting and providing ongoing maintenance and enhancements to tax calculation software
- Translating complex Brazilian tax legislation into technical requirements for Engineering, Applied Science, and Product teams
- Providing support to the end-to-end Global transaction tax calculation solutions for a broad array of products and services
- Supporting internal and external Audits
- Providing technical advice on indirect tax rates applicable to Amazon's catalogue based on the research of tax laws, regulations and jurisdiction changes
- Supporting local business partners and tax department members with decisions on product taxability matters
- Participating in team operational excellence programs and process improvements
- Actively participate in selected projects throughout their life-cycle
