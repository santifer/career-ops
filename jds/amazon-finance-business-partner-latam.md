Finance is all about the numbers...unless you work at Amazon; it's knowing the numbers and so much more. Enthusiasm and energy help us deliver new ideas, and solutions. Do you view problems as treasures, and are you willing to dive deep to develop those solutions and deliver results? Will you seek to challenge the status quo, and accept that your ideas and mechanisms may be tested daily?

### Key job responsibilities

- Financial modeling, providing analytical support, and validating financial and operational results of Operating Expenses and Sales productivity.
- Ensure a high level of financial controls are followed, maintaining data integrity.
- Support on forecasting and P&L reviews
- Controllership of Marketplace International Sales for Latam, understanding actuals, challenging variances and tracking action plans to bring financial results back to plan when necessary and possible
- Drive the decisions on Operating Expenses and Sales productivity, driving efficiencies together with an aligned business growth strategy
- Drive the implementation and best use of global best practices
- Orchestrate the whole planning process, from aligning assumptions and providing analysis to system load and data integrity checks
- Provide accurate and insightful financial reports
- Find opportunities to use global standards and transfer repetitive tasks to global teams
- Develop financial skills across the organization
- Build great talent inside and outside Finance
