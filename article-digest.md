- AEO strategy centred on **query fan-out**: identifying related questions AI search systems generate from broad prompts
- Research inputs: Google Search Console query data, "people also search for" results, social/forum discussions, repeated customer questions
- Content restructured for answer engines: direct answers at top, clear headers, FAQ sections, bullet points, tables, concise recommendations from actual product usage
- Original data from real wedding couples' question submissions and observed user behaviour (e.g., recommending 10–12 game questions and a 10–12 minute game length)
