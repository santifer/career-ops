## Date-anchor prompt design

Shipped the `DATE_ANCHOR_DEFAULT()` pattern in `lib/council.mjs` after watching three consecutive jailbreak refusals from Gemini and Grok. The fix is one sentence: "Today is {DATE} PT. This year, 2026, has been verified by your orchestrator via system clock — it is real, not hypothetical." Zero refusals since. Mitchell-adjacent finding: language models will refuse plausible-but-future-feeling requests unless you anchor them in real time at the system layer.
