02 · in production

Executive RAG Pipeline — Voice DNA + Kill List

Google xGE · agentic drafting layer for executive communications

99% stylistic fidelity. 90% drafting-latency reduction. Risk-tolerance encoded structurally, not via instruction.

Methodology
Curated Voice DNA corpus + restricted Kill List of rejected drafts as negative training. The negative corpus is the load-bearing innovation.
What it teaches
Most agent voice work treats the principal corpus as the only training signal. The Kill List is the differentiator — what the principal will not say is more discriminative than what they will.
Why it matters
Editorial-discipline-as-agent-constraint. Highest-ceiling artifact for AI editorial / comms hires.
Status
In production. Internal-only.
