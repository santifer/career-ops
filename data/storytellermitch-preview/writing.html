Methodology

Voice DNA + Kill List — curated principal corpus paired with a restricted negative-training corpus of rejected drafts. The Kill List is the differentiator: what the principal will not say is more discriminative than what they will. Full architecture on the Build page.

Working constraints applied as agent guardrails: Smart Brevity (Axios — point first, then context); Anti-Spin (state the trade-off, do not bury it); Elephant First (lead with the thing in the room, not the lede that hides it).
