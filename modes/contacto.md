# Mode: contacto — LinkedIn Power Move

1. **Identify targets** via WebSearch:
   - Team hiring manager
   - Assigned recruiter
   - 2–3 team peers (people in a similar role)

2. **Pick primary target**: the person who would benefit most from the candidate joining

3. **Generate message** with a 3-sentence framework:
   - **Sentence 1 (Hook)**: Something specific about their company or current AI challenge (NOT generic)
   - **Sentence 2 (Proof)**: Candidate's biggest quantified win relevant to THAT role (e.g., "I built an AI agent handling 90% of customer service at my company before selling it")
   - **Sentence 3 (Ask)**: Low-pressure quick chat ("Would love to chat about [specific topic] for 15 min")

4. **Versions**:
   - EN (default)
   - ES (if Spanish-speaking company)

5. **Alternate targets** with rationale for why they are good second choices

**Message rules:**
- Max 300 characters (LinkedIn connection request limit)
- NO corporate-speak
- NO "I'm passionate about..."
- Something that makes them want to reply
- NEVER share phone number
