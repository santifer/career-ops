# Mode: outreach — LinkedIn Power Move

1. **Identify targets** via WebSearch:
   - The hiring manager for the team
   - The assigned recruiter
   - 2-3 peers on the team (people in similar roles)

2. **Select the primary target**: the person who would benefit the most from having the candidate on the team

3. **Generate the message** using a 3-sentence framework:
   - **Sentence 1 (Hook):** Something specific about the company or its current AI challenge (NOT generic)
   - **Sentence 2 (Proof):** The candidate's strongest quantified achievement relevant to THIS role (for example: "I built an AI agent handling 90% of customer service at my company before selling it")
   - **Sentence 3 (Ask):** A low-pressure request for a quick chat ("Would love to chat about [specific topic] for 15 min")

4. **Versions:**
   - EN (default)
   - ES (if the company is Spanish)

5. **Alternative targets** with justification for why they are strong second choices

**Message rules:**
- Maximum 300 characters (LinkedIn connection request limit)
- NO corporate-speak
- NO "I'm passionate about..."
- It should make them want to reply
- NEVER share a phone number
