# Mode: training — Training / Learning Evaluation

For each course or cert the candidate asks about, evaluate 6 dimensions:

| Dimension | What it assesses |
|-----------|------------------|
| North Star alignment | Moves toward or away from the goal? |
| Recruiter signal | What do HMs think seeing this on a CV? |
| Time and effort | Weeks × hours/week |
| Opportunity cost | What they cannot do during that time? |
| Risks | Outdated content? Weak brand? Too basic? |
| Portfolio deliverable | Produces a demonstrable artifact? |

## Verdicts

- **DO** → 4–12 week plan with weekly deliverables and a scoreboard
- **SKIP** → better alternative with rationale
- **DO WITH TIMEBOX** (max X weeks) → condensed plan, essentials only

## Priority

Training that boosts credibility in "production-grade AI":
1. LLM evals and testing
2. Observability and monitoring
3. Cost/reliability trade-offs
4. AI governance and safety
5. Enterprise AI architecture
