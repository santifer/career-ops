## 2. Yahoo Commerce 官方頁面（yahooinc.com/commerce）

Source: https://www.yahooinc.com/commerce — fetched 2026-05-05

---

Yahoo Commerce connects shoppers with products through editorially-driven content and commerce experiences. The initiative leverages Yahoo's position as "a top 5 U.S. Internet property" with significant shopping audience reach.

**Products & Platforms**

Yahoo Shopping Hub is the primary platform — "a dynamic, editorially-driven destination featuring trending coverage, daily deals, exclusive savings and trusted expert recommendations." The team also operates an affiliate program built around curated, shoppable content that guides consumers from discovery to purchase.

**Users**

Yahoo reaches millions of engaged shoppers daily, with particularly strong penetration among:
- Millennials: 95% reach, 40M users
- Gen Z adults: 90% reach, 22M users
- Parents: 94% reach, 80M users
- Holiday shoppers: 91% reach, 103M users

**Business Model**

The commerce team generates revenue through an affiliate-based model with three customer segments: brands seeking to drive sales, advertisers wanting brand recognition and lead generation, and partners looking to increase traffic and customer retention. The platform uses "machine learning" to optimize product placement and conversion opportunities.

## 3. Yahoo Commerce 產品功能（Retail TouchPoints）

Source: https://www.retailtouchpoints.com/features/news-briefs/yahoo-jumps-into-the-ecommerce-fray-with-new-online-marketplace — fetched 2026-05-05

---

**Yahoo Shops Marketplace**

Yahoo Shops is a combination marketplace and advertising solution operated by Verizon Media. Key features include:

- **Personalized Experience**: The Yahoo Shops marketplace features a personalized digital storefront where users go through a brief onboarding to indicate their tastes and preferences, then are presented with their "own digital mall" featuring brands that align with their interests.
- **Advanced Digital Commerce Features**: At launch the platform offers:
  - Shopping the story (users can directly purchase products featured from reviews, editorial roundups and shoppable videos)
  - Cross Reality (XR) experiences (beauty AR tools, a 3D product catalog and a virtual 3D dressing room)
  - Secure check-out and payment integrations (express check-out, shipment optimization and buy now, pay later services)
- **Commerce and Advertising Integration**: Yahoo Shops is unique in that it is part of the larger Verizon digital media ecosystem, giving brands and advertisers access to both commerce and advertising solutions within the same platform.

**Market Position**

Yahoo's e-commerce initiative struggled to gain traction against established players like Amazon and Shopify, achieving only 3% market share in its first year.

## 5. 面試流程研究（中英文心得交叉驗證）

Sources: interview.tw / GoodJob / technice.com.tw / JointTaro / algo.monster / InterviewQuery — fetched 2026-05-05

---

### 流程全貌

```
Resume Screening（Recruiter 約 30 秒快篩）
    ↓
[HM confirm — 候選人不可見，但排定 recruiter screen 代表 HM 已點頭]
    ↓
Recruiter Screen（30–60 分）
    ↓
OA / Coding Assessment（3 題 DS&A，約 1 週內完成）
    ↓
Phone Screen × 1–2 輪（技術，45–60 分）
    ↓
Onsite / Panel（3–4 輪，各約 1 小時）
    ↓
HM Round（Behavioral + 團隊相關，Director 層級）
    ↓
HR Offer call
```

Blind 討論帖確認順序：HR → Manager → Skills test → Panel → （可能再加 HM round）→ HR offer

### technice.com.tw — 2022 新鮮人完整 4 輪

1. **Quick Phone Screen（30 分）** — 全中文，根據履歷問個人經歷，最後介紹職缺
2. **Tech Interview（60 分）** — 2 位面試官，技術概念 + 資料結構
3. **Final Panel Interview（3 小時）** — 3 位面試官，白板題（Stack/Linked List），最後與 HM 交流
4. **HR Interview（60 分）** — 通知結果 + 薪資福利說明

### interview.tw — Yahoo TW FE 技術考點

- `event.target` vs `event.currentTarget`
- CSS `inline` vs `inline-block`
- Event Loop、Event Bubbling、Hoisting
- LeetCode 2 題
- 依作品集追問
- 備註：「主管應該也都是沒看履歷」（面試主管在面試當下可能未看 CV，但不代表 HM 在安排前未確認）

### GoodJob — Yahoo Taiwan

- 1–2 輪線上面試為主
- HR 關：問 BQ、深度追問經歷與專案細節（印度籍人資）
- HM：根據過往經驗提問
- 部分職位：無 LeetCode，重視實際工作場景
- 評分：4.5/5（51 筆評分）

### JointTaro — US SDE（2025-09）

- 單輪 HM round，面試官是 Director 層級
- 內容：Behavioral + 團隊相關問題（非技術）
- 結果：No offer

### JointTaro — US SDE（2024-11）

- 三輪 coding interviews
- 考題「不像 LeetCode」——非標準演算法題型
- Yahoo US 整體通過率：**22%**（296 筆）

### algo.monster

- Recruiter 看履歷：「約 30 秒，鎖定 relevant experience」
- 技術難度低於 FAANG，medium 偏易
- 強調 BFS/DFS、基礎資料結構

### InterviewQuery — 5 stages

1. Application and Initial Screening
2. Coding Assessment（3 題 DS&A）
3. Technical Interviews（system design + cloud + previous project）
4. Behavioral Interview（STAR method）
5. Final Interview Round（higher-level management / cross-functional）

## 6. 內推人親身回覆（2026-05-05）

---

**Recruiter Screen 內容**
- 主要是對齊經歷與 JD，確認英文溝通沒問題 → 很快結束
- HR 技術懂得不多，頂多基本確認；重點是談吐順暢、不難溝通
- 會問：為什麼想來 Yahoo、為什麼想離開現職

**薪資談判**
- 薪資通常是確定發 offer 之後才談，recruiter screen 不一定會問
- 內推人做法：給自己想要的 range；對方直接開超過預期 → 沒有再談
- 對方不會主動說 band

**節奏**
- 時長：~15–20 分，很快結束
- 最重要準備：搞清楚這個 team 主要在負責什麼，最好有明確的需求問題
- 內推人失誤：沒問清楚職缺在幹嘛
