# Interview Prep Context

`interview-prep/` is user layer. It stores company-specific interview preparation and the accumulated story bank.

`story-bank.md` is the long-term source for reusable STAR+R stories. STAR+R means Situation, Task, Action, Result, and Reflection.

When evaluations uncover strong proof points or interview stories, add them here only if they are reusable and grounded in the user's actual CV, profile, reports, or supplied context.

Do not invent achievements. If a story needs a metric that is not in the source material, mark it as a question for the user instead of fabricating it.
