Brian self-assesses at 3-4 years of solid PM experience, not 5+. He knows he should push himself but feels the gap when roles require 5+ years.

**How to apply:** When evaluating roles that require 5+ years, flag the gap honestly in the report and in the score — don't paper over it with "co-founder experience counts." Surface it as a real gap, note whether the JD has language like "if your experience is close, apply anyway," and let Brian decide. Don't systematically oversell seniority.
