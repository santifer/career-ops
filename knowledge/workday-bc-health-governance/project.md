## Business Context

British Columbia's health system consists of seven regional health authorities operating 300+ hospitals and health clinics serving 220,000+ employees. Historically, these organizations operated on different enterprise systems, creating inefficiencies in payroll, HR, financial reporting, and resource planning.

The initiative: Migrate all seven health organizations to a unified Workday platform. This was not a typical IT implementation — it required coordinating across organizational silos, managing political relationships between health authorities, aligning provincial and regional leadership, and managing organizational change at unprecedented scale.

**Key Challenges:**
- Political complexity: Seven independent health authorities with different cultures and priorities
- Organizational scale: 220,000+ end users affected; change management required across entire public sector health system
- Technical integration: Consolidating different HR, payroll, and financial systems onto single platform
- Governance: Creating decision-making structures that ensured alignment without creating bottlenecks
