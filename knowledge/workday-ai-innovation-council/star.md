# Workday AI Innovation Council — STAR Framework (Interview/External)

## SITUATION

The Workday Healthcare practice was delivering complex, mission-critical implementations to large hospital networks and healthcare systems. Consultant productivity was a bottleneck — projects required large teams and long timelines, and there were opportunities to use AI to accelerate delivery.

While AI was being discussed as a strategic priority at Workday, implementation was fragmented. Different practices were experimenting with AI independently, without systematic evaluation of impact or coordination of resources.

The opportunity: Create an elite team to systematically identify, build, and deploy AI use cases that would measurably improve consultant productivity, accelerate project delivery, and reduce staffing requirements.

---

## TASK

Recruited into an elite, highly selective team within the Healthcare practice (only 2 people per vertical nominated for this role, based on demonstrated AI capability and track record):

- **Identify high-impact AI use cases** — Work with consultants, program leadership, and the Community of Practice to surface opportunities where AI could meaningfully improve productivity
- **Build reusable AI assets** — Develop solutions that could be deployed across multiple client programs and consultants
- **Drive adoption at scale** — Ensure that built assets were adopted widely, not left as isolated pilots
- **Measure and optimize** — Track productivity improvements, iterate on solutions, and build the business case for continued investment
