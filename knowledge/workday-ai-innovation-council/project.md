# Workday AI Innovation Council — Project Details

## Overview

Member of an elite, selective team within Workday's Healthcare practice tasked with identifying, building, and deploying high-impact AI use cases. The Innovation Council operated as the research and development arm of the Healthcare practice, defining AI use cases that made consultants more productive and enabling them to deliver faster, leaner, and with smaller teams.

## Business Context

Workday Professional Services operates as a practice delivering enterprise transformation and deployment services to customers. The Healthcare vertical works with large healthcare systems and hospital networks on Workday platform implementations.

The Innovation Council was established to identify and deploy AI use cases that improved consultant productivity and project delivery efficiency. Rather than building customer-facing AI products, the Innovation Council created internal consulting efficiency tools that accelerated project execution, reduced staffing requirements, and improved delivery quality.

## What Was Built

### Council Function and Intake Process

**Role within Healthcare Practice**
- Member of elite team defining and deploying AI use cases
- Worked alongside Product, Sales, and Customer Success teams
- Served as bridge between emerging AI capabilities and consultant productivity challenges

**Use Case Identification & Development Pipeline**
- Received use case submissions from the Community of Practice (see related project)
- Evaluated proposed use cases for impact, feasibility, and scalability
- Prioritized use cases based on potential productivity gains and adoption likelihood
- Built and deployed selected use cases across the Healthcare practice

**Asset Development**
- Produced 10+ reusable AI assets designed to improve consultant productivity
- Assets addressed core consultant pain points in implementation delivery
- Each asset built to be adopted across multiple client programs

### Impact on Consultant Productivity

The Innovation Council's AI assets were deployed across Workday's largest and most complex customer programs:

**Productivity Improvements**
- Up to 30% increase in consultant productivity on programs using deployed assets
- Enabled consultants to complete tasks faster without sacrificing quality or depth
- Reduced time spent on routine activities, freeing capacity for higher-value consulting work

**Organizational Impact**
- 7% reduction in total consulting hours across ALL verticals in Professional Services org-wide
- This organization-wide reduction came from an innovation initiative within a single vertical (Healthcare)
- Demonstrates ripple effect of effective AI tooling across the practice

**Program-Level Adoption**
- 10+ reusable AI assets adopted on approximately 80% of Workday's largest and most complex customer programs
- Assets became standard tooling for Healthcare practice deployments
- High adoption rate indicates product-market fit and genuine consultant value
