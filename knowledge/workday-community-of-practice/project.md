# Workday Community of Practice — Project Details

## Overview

Established and managed the Healthcare practice's AI Community of Practice — a cross-functional intake mechanism that surfaced, evaluated, and prioritized grassroots AI experiments and use cases across the entire Healthcare vertical. Served as the bridge between frontline consultants experimenting with AI and the AI Innovation Council's research and development efforts.

**Key Function:** Bottom-up innovation intake and curation

---

## Business Context

As AI became a strategic priority for Workday Professional Services, consultants across different practices began experimenting independently with AI tools and approaches. These experiments were valuable signals of where AI could improve delivery — but without coordination, they remained scattered pilots.

The Healthcare practice recognized the opportunity to systematize this bottom-up innovation. Rather than mandate AI from the top down, the Community of Practice created a structured mechanism for:
- Capturing grassroots AI experiments and use cases
- Surfacing promising ideas to leadership and the Innovation Council
- Connecting isolated pilot projects into a coordinated innovation pipeline
- Creating feedback loops from deployment back to the broader community

---

## What Was Built

### Community of Practice Function

**Intake and Surfacing Mechanism**
- Established regular forums for consultants and program leaders to share AI experiments and ideas
- Created simple, low-friction processes for proposing and documenting use cases
- Built visibility into ongoing AI work across Healthcare practice teams
- Aggregated use case proposals for prioritization and evaluation

**Curation and Prioritization**
- Evaluated incoming use case proposals against criteria:
  - Magnitude of consultant pain point being addressed
  - Potential for productivity improvement
  - Feasibility and effort required to build solution
  - Scalability across programs and teams
- Prioritized use cases for escalation to AI Innovation Council

**Connection to Innovation Council Pipeline**
- Served as intake funnel feeding Innovation Council's development pipeline
- Communicated Innovation Council priorities back to Community of Practice
- Supported consultants in contributing to Innovation Council-backed projects
- Created feedback loops from Innovation Council deployments back to community

### Organizational Design

**Membership and Participation**
- Membership included consultants, program leadership, innovation team members
- Regular meetings and communication channels fostering cross-team collaboration
- Low barrier to entry encouraging broad participation

**Governance and Escalation**
- Clear criteria for evaluating and prioritizing use cases
- Transparent escalation path from Community to Innovation Council
- Regular updates on status of proposed use cases
- Feedback loops ensuring contributors understood what happened to their ideas

## Innovation Pipeline & Workflow

### Three-Tier Model

**1. Community of Practice (Intake & Surfacing)**
- Broad participation from consultants and program teams
- Encourages experimentation and idea generation
- Low-friction proposal process
- Continuous intake of new ideas and emerging use cases

**2. AI Innovation Council (Evaluation & Development)**
- Elite team evaluating Community of Practice proposals
- Prioritizes highest-impact opportunities
- Builds and tests solutions
- Develops 10+ reusable AI assets

**3. Practice Deployment (Scaling & Adoption)**
- Innovation Council assets deployed to broader Healthcare practice
- Becomes standard tooling and best practices
- Drives measurable productivity improvements
- Creates proof points for next round of innovation

### Feedback Loop Structure

- Community surfaces use cases → Innovation Council evaluates → Council develops solutions → Solutions deployed to practice → Community provides feedback on adoption and impact → Cycle continues

## Relationship to AI Innovation Council

The Community of Practice and AI Innovation Council form a complete innovation system:

- **Community of Practice** provides structured intake of ideas and use cases from the broad consultant base
- **Innovation Council** evaluates, prioritizes, and builds the most impactful solutions
- **Practice deployment** scales successful solutions to broader organization
- **Feedback loops** ensure Community of Practice understands what solutions have been deployed and their impact

Without the Community of Practice, Innovation Council would lack credible, bottom-up input on where to focus efforts. Without the Innovation Council, Community ideas would remain scattered pilots. Together, they create a systematic innovation pipeline.
