# Deloitte Digital Transformation Roadmap
## Project Details

### Timeline
- **Duration**: Approximately 2 years (July 2016 – ~2018)
- **Phase**: Early career at Deloitte Consulting; foundational work in digital transformation strategy practice

### Client & Context
- **Client Type**: Risk management company (Fortune 500/mid-market)
- **Engagement Scope**: Full-scope digital transformation assessment across entire enterprise
- **Client Complexity**: Multi-business-unit organization with diverse system landscapes (asset management, finance, procurement, operations)

### Project Scope

#### Objective
Conduct a comprehensive digital capability assessment across all business units, identify high-value transformation opportunities, and develop a detailed 5-year implementation roadmap with sequencing, vendor solutions, and expected ROI for each workstream.

#### Methodology & Approach

**Current State Assessment**:
- Mapped every material technology system across every business unit
- Documented system functionality, data flows, integration points, and technical debt
- Assessed operational workflows and process maturity

**Industry Benchmark Analysis**:
- Compared current state against industry-standard process blueprints
- Identified capability gaps between current state and best-in-class operations
- Quantified maturity levels across business functions

**Gap Analysis & Valuation**:
- Systematically identified all technology, process, and capability gaps
- Quantified economic impact for each identified gap (if we implement X technology, we achieve Y operational savings/revenue enablement)
- Prioritized gaps by financial return on investment and strategic importance
- Built business case for addressing each gap

**Vendor & Solution Strategy**:
- Conducted vendor qualification process for all identified solution categories
- Built shortlist of qualified vendors for each product category
- Evaluated vendors on functionality, implementation risk, total cost of ownership, and strategic fit
- Recommended specific solutions for each major workstream

**Roadmap Development**:
- Built detailed 5-year implementation roadmap showing deployment sequence
- Sequenced projects to manage organizational change, resource constraints, and integration dependencies
- Included phasing for technology enablement (Year 1, Year 2, etc.)
- Defined success metrics and benefits realization approach

#### Deliverables

**Strategic Documents**:
- Comprehensive digital transformation assessment report
- Current-state system landscape documentation
- Industry benchmark analysis and gap assessment
- Total cost of ownership (TCO) analysis for recommended solutions
- 5-year deployment roadmap with phasing and sequencing
- Business case with ROI projections by workstream

**Commercial Outputs**:
- Proposal documents for each identified workstream
- Vendor shortlist and recommendation memos
- Implementation approach and resource plan
- Change management and organizational readiness assessment

### Impact & Outcomes

**Deal Value**:
- **Year 1 Implementation**: Secured $30M converted deal for initial deployment phase
- **Identified ROI**: $31M in annual recurring revenue from digital capability improvements
- **Total Pipeline**: Roadmap identified additional opportunities beyond Year 1

**Strategic Influence**:
- **Assessment Framework Adoption**: The assessment methodology and framework became the North Star (template and standard approach) for Deloitte's entire Technology Strategy practice
- **Repeatable Model**: Other engagement teams adopted this framework as the standard for conducting digital transformation assessments with new clients
- **Practice Leverage**: Framework became billable IP that enabled Deloitte to scale similar engagements across the firm

**Market Impact**:
- Directly enabled Deloitte to win massive SAP implementation contracts (north of $50M) with this and related clients
- Established Deloitte's credibility in digital transformation strategy for financial services and risk management sectors
- Created the foundation for Jon's subsequent involvement in large-scale SAP implementations (including Chevron engagement)

### Capability Clusters

**Primary Clusters**:
- **Product Strategy & Go-to-Market**: Vendor evaluation, solution architecture, roadmap development
- **Operational Scaling & Team Design**: Large-scale transformation planning, multi-workstream coordination, organizational change management

### Learning & Career Trajectory

This engagement served as the cornerstone for Jon's transition into strategic consulting. The assessment framework became his intellectual property within Deloitte and enabled him to lead subsequent large-scale enterprise transformation engagements, ultimately leading to selection as a key resource on the Chevron Fortune 10 engagement.
