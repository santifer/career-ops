## Day 1 — AI Foundations, Value Framing & C-Suite Storytelling

### Core AI Concepts Covered
- AI taxonomy: Traditional AI → Machine Learning → Deep Learning → Generative AI → Agentic AI
- Agents: Autonomous systems that plan, decide, and execute multi-step tasks using generative models with memory, tool use, and feedback loops
- Model Context Protocol (MCP): Standard interface for agents to securely connect to enterprise systems, data sources, and tools — "API for AI"
- Data flows, orchestration, and workflow integration
- Agent capabilities vs. LLMs: planning, memory, tool integration, real-time data, self-assessment

### Executive Communication Frameworks
- **Value-First Approach:** Frame AI around P&L impact, risk reduction, and business outcomes — not features
- **Executive-Specific Framing:**
  - **CIO:** Platform modernization, AI CoE, cross-system agents, security/governance
  - **CFO:** Close acceleration, forecast confidence, controls/audit automation, spend visibility
  - **CHRO:** Employee experience, talent acquisition, internal mobility, AI enablement
  - **COO:** Guided buying, contract intelligence, frontline operations, supplier risk
- **Discovery Questions:** Tailored question sets per executive persona to surface AI priorities

### Key Talking Points
- "Don't sell the features. Sell the business outcomes relevant to the specific executive and their priorities."
- "The question isn't 'Is GenAI perfect today?' It's 'Will you be ready when it's good enough and cheap enough for your competitors?'"
- "Frame AI as a moving frontier, not a static feature."

## Why Illuminate Tools Are Not Being Adopted (key insight from training)

Identified barriers to Workday AI feature adoption:
- Organizational bureaucracy (customer approval processes slow adoption)
- Compliance concerns on data usage and risk
- Required effort by customer to adopt
- No or limited perceived value by customer
- No ML/AI consideration during deployment (partners don't raise it)
- Limited insight/best practice from Workday CSMs to drive adoption
