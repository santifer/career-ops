# Griz AI Creative Agency — STAR Framework (Interview/External)

## SITUATION

Joined Griz as Fractional COO during a critical growth phase. The company had proven market fit with enterprise clients (Nike, Adidas, Shopify, Mercedes-Benz) but lacked operational infrastructure and scalable delivery capabilities. The market was moving rapidly toward AI-powered creative workflows, but the company's tools and processes were built for traditional media.

The challenge: Scale delivery capacity, operationalize processes across multiple concurrent client engagements, and evolve from manual media optimization to fully automated AI-driven creative production — all while maintaining quality and client satisfaction.

---

## TASK

Built two parallel systems to unlock the company's growth and competitive potential:

**Operating System Track:** Established operational infrastructure enabling predictable, scalable delivery
- Cross-functional process architecture connecting strategy, creative, performance, and iteration
- Business development and sales frameworks driving repeatable customer acquisition
- Resource optimization and project management infrastructure

**AI/Tech Track:** Evolved the technology stack from traditional media optimization to fully AI-native creative production
- Architected end-to-end AI creative pipeline (generation + optimization)
- Built centralized analytics platform for real-time performance measurement and iteration
- Designed intelligent optimization engine enabling rapid, data-driven creative improvements

---

## ACTION

### Operational Scaling (33% Capacity Increase)

**Business Development System**
- Mapped ideal customer profile and designed prospect qualification framework
- Built prospecting playbook with outreach templates, pitch frameworks, and sales collateral
- Created standardized customer delivery framework (intake → scoping → kickoff → ongoing management)
- Established reusable engagement templates across common use cases
- **Result:** Repeatable sales process driving 35% revenue growth; predictable pipeline

**Operational Infrastructure**
- Designed comprehensive project management framework with capacity planning, resource allocation, timeline management
- Built workflow documentation and process templates enabling repeatable delivery
- Established governance and escalation protocols ensuring quality across projects
- **Result:** Handled 33% increase in concurrent client projects without proportional staffing increase; 15% improvement in campaign delivery timelines

### AI/Technology Evolution (End-to-End AI Creative Pipeline)

**Phase 1: Traditional Media Optimization**
- Started with AI-driven performance analysis of client-created assets
- Measured hooks, hold rates, influencer performance, click-through rates
- Built diagnostic capabilities identifying underperforming creative elements

**Phase 2: Intelligent Iteration Engine**
- Developed AI tools determining when iterations were necessary
- Built automated feedback mechanisms informing creative teams where changes were needed
- Enabled rapid iteration cycles based on real-time performance data
- **Result:** 25% improvement in creative iteration efficiency

**Phase 3: End-to-End AI Native Pipeline** *(Breakthrough Achievement)*
- Architected fully AI-generated and AI-optimized creative production system
- Deployed centralized data platform ingesting real-time user-generated analytics
- Built performance tracking system revealing which hooks, influencers, and audience segments performed best
- Implemented automated asset editing capabilities driving rapid iterations on underperforming elements
- Integrated AI generation and AI optimization into seamless workflow

**Flagship Example: Adidas Campaign**
- AI-generated video and visual assets from creative brief
- Real-time measurement of engagement across audience segments and influencers
- Automated hook detection revealing which creative elements resonated with target audiences
- Continuous iteration cycles: performance data → asset modifications → re-measurement → further optimization
- Measurable performance improvements at each iteration

**Result:** Cut turnaround times by 15%; raised campaign success rates by 25%; established Griz as leader in AI-powered creative production
