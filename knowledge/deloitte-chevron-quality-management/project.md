## Products Built

### Product Portfolio Overview
Four custom applications deployed on SAP Business Technology Platform (cloud extensibility layer) that automated and optimized the quality testing process:

1. **Raw Material Inspection & Tolerance Validation**
   - Automated inspection of incoming raw materials (trains, barges, rail shipments)
   - Real-time validation against tolerance specifications
   - Automated rejection workflow for out-of-spec materials
   - Prevented contamination of finished products

2. **Inspection Plan Management**
   - Centralized inspection plan specifications by material type and vendor
   - Defined quality metrics and tolerance thresholds
   - Automated routing of inspection tasks to lab and field teams

3. **Finished Product Quality & Blend Validation**
   - Post-production quality inspection for finished products
   - Automated validation against blend optimization recipes
   - Tracked first-time pass rates and quality holds
   - Supported complex multi-material blending scenarios

4. **Quality Holds & Exception Management**
   - Automated escalation of out-of-spec materials and products
   - Workflow routing for quality holds resolution
   - Real-time visibility into quality issues and root causes

### Business Process Impact

**Quality Testing Process:**
- **Raw Materials Phase:** Materials arrive via multiple transport modes → automated inspection against tolerance specs → validation against vendor-specific inspection plans → acceptance or rejection
- **Production Phase:** Raw materials blended per optimization recipes → finished products inspected against blend specifications
- **Intermediate Products:** Raw materials that become intermediate products (used in downstream finished products) tracked with lineage and quality metadata

**Quality Outcomes:**
- Faster inspection cycles (reduced time-to-release)
- Fewer quality holds (improved production continuity)
- Higher first-time blend rates (fewer reworks)
- Higher quality finished products (fewer customer returns, better margin protection)

## Adoption Strategy & Execution

### The Challenge
- End users: Blue collar refinery workers on 24/7 shift cycles
- User demographic: Limited software experience, hands-on production focus
- Adoption barrier: Complex quality workflows, change management resistance, shift-work constraints

### Adoption Approach

**Field Engagement (4 AM Shifts):**
- Embedded with shift workers across all 24-hour cycles
- Observed actual work processes in production environment
- Conducted hands-on training at point of use
- Gathered real-time feedback and pain points

**Feedback-Driven Iteration:**
- Built KPI frameworks tracking behavioral usage patterns
- Captured user feedback through surveys, observation notes, usage analytics
- Deployed rapid iteration cycles: feedback → product changes → redeployment
- Created closed-loop feedback mechanism for continuous improvement

**Change Management:**
- Worked with shift supervisors and area leads as change champions
- Addressed concerns specific to 24/7 operations
- Provided shift-specific training and documentation
- Monitored adoption metrics across shifts and refineries
